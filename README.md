# RideSphere - Vehicle Rental System

Welcome to RideSphere, your ultimate vehicle rental system designed to offer seamless, efficient, and user-friendly vehicle rental services. Whether you need a car for a business trip, a family vacation, or just to get around town, RideSphere has you covered.

## Features

- **Wide Range of Vehicles:** Choose from a diverse selection of vehicles, from compact cars to luxury SUVs.
- **Affordable Prices:** Competitive rental rates providing you with the best value for your money.
- **Easy Booking Process:** Enjoy a seamless booking experience with our user-friendly platform.
- **24/7 Customer Support:** Our dedicated team is available around the clock to assist you with any queries or issues.
- **Secure Payments:** Safe and secure payment options for a hassle-free transaction.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
- **Backend:** Flask (Python)
- **Database:** SQLite
- **Version Control:** Git
